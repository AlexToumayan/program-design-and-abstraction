An Introduction to Computer Science
Alex Toumayan

A ground-up reconstruction of the classic Composing Programs curriculum, written for the curious beginner. Each lesson opens with an everyday analogy, develops the formal idea with care, traces the machinery by hand, and ends by checking your understanding — building, chapter by chapter, from a single expression to a working interpreter.
